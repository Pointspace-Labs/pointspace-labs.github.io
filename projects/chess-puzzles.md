# Chess Puzzle Generator

This project generates chess puzzles across multiple difficulty tiers.

## Demo
- **Play puzzles:** [Open the demo](chess-puzzles/demo/)

## What it does
- Generates tactical puzzles from candidate positions
- Verifies solutions and filters low-quality puzzles
- Assigns difficulty tiers (high-level method described here)
